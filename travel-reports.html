Travel Reports

Vacation in Ireland

Welcome to the Cliffs of Moher! The breathtaking nature in Ireland has captivated us all. The cliffs by the sea are truly impressive and offer a fantastic view over the water. We enjoyed the fresh sea air and let the beauty of the landscape sink in. It was an unforgettable experience that we will always remember. Hard to believe, but some people will do anything for a good photo. Climbing down the slopes is life-threatening and not worth any picture in the world. There is plenty to marvel at as it is. Rarely has a landscape impressed me this much.

Denmark - We Had a Nice Time!

Our visit to Denmark was truly wonderful! The beautiful landscape and the friendly locals left a great impression on us. We spent a lot of time at the harbor and discovered the local culture. The tranquility really helped us to finally wind down. What was both pleasant and startling at the same time? The absolute silence. No vehicle noise. No noise from any machinery. We would love to come back.

Germany - We Stayed Home

Instead of flying again, we decided to spend our time in Germany. The natural beauty and the quiet atmosphere have greatly impressed us. We spent a lot of time in nature. Finally, I could visit the Partnachklamm, which has been on my list for a long time. The photo can hardly capture how impressive the gorge really is. Whoever gets the chance should definitely visit it. It goes to show that there are incredibly beautiful places right here in Germany as well.
